How to create NFT on Polygon Blockchain :

1. Open OpenSea's website.
2. Link Meta Mask polygon wallet with opensea website.
3. Click on Create button.
4. Then from drop down menu select My collections.
5. Fill all details and description and also upload file.
6. After that create NFT and verify captcha code and your NFT added to your collection.

How to sell an NFT on Ethereum Blockchain :

1. Select NFT and click on the Sell button then get a price page.
2. Now, enter any fixed auction price then select royality bonus on the NFT.
3. Then confirm your setting and put NFT on sale.
4. We will get notification, if anyone decide to buy NFT.
